Vanilla survival server set on hard difficulty.

Server rules:

  • No using a hacked or modified client to exploit or gain any unfair advantages in the game!
  • No griefing or stealing from anyone on the server!! Breaking this rule will get you kicked off of the server!
  • No maliciously PvP'in! All battles must be agreed upon with all participants!

Datapacks

  • Durability Ping: Alerts the player when your tools get to 2% durability.
  • More Mob Heads: Adds a chance for mob heads to drop upon death.
  • Player Head Drops: Player heads will drop when a player is killed by another player, and it will display who the killer is.
  • Multiplayer Sleep: 1 player skip the night.
  • Coords Hud: Adds coords, direction and time of day to your screen "trigger ch_toggle"
  • Nether Portal Coords: Use "Trigger nc_inNether" or "Trigger nc_inOverworld" See this video for more help
